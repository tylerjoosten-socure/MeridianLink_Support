Complete at first touch

Purpose: Complete this template for any Socure-related support case before working or escalating it. Capturing complete information upfront reduces back-and-forth, speeds resolution, and ensures anything escalated to Socure arrives with everything the SME team needs.

How to use it: Tier 1/Tier 2 fills in every applicable field. Fields marked ● are required before routing to Socure. Submit Socure cases through the Salesforce support portal (not chat/phone). Use the When-to-Escalate guide at the top of this page to decide whether the case escalates at all.

When you're done, use Download PDF or Email Support at the bottom. The PDF carries a machine-readable JSON copy of every field as an embedded attachment.

When to Escalate (and when NOT to)

Read this first. Rule of thumb: escalate when it requires changing how decisions are made or a deep fraud investigation; otherwise handle within MeridianLink or route to the AE.
Escalation reference guide
✓ Escalate to Socure
  • Transaction deep dive — complex investigation of a specific transaction
  • Enhancement / feature requests
  • API failures (sometimes — confirm it isn't environment/integration first)
  • Outages or widespread errors
  • Deep logic recommendation exercises — route to Socure via Matt Green
✕ Do NOT escalate
  • "What triggered the decline" — explain via dashboard reason codes & Explainability
  • Not displaying in MeridianLink but does in Socure — MeridianLink display/integration issue
  • Customer wants to add products / commercial questions — route to Account Executive
  • Dashboard permission / role access — route to AE (config on Socure side, escalated to Matt Green)
  • Policy-driven decisions — customer owns their decision logic & thresholds
